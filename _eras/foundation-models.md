GPT-3's arrival in 2020 demonstrated that scaling language models produced emergent, surprising capabilities, and ChatGPT's 2022 launch brought AI into mainstream daily life almost overnight. Foundation models reshaped industries from medicine to law while igniting urgent global debates about safety, alignment, and regulation. The question shifted from whether machines could think to what humanity should do now that they increasingly seem to.
