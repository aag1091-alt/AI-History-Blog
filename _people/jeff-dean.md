Jeffrey Adgate Dean (born July 23, 1968) is an American computer scientist who built much of the software backbone that powers Google at planetary scale—from MapReduce and BigTable to TensorFlow. He has led Google AI since 2018 and became Google's chief scientist in 2023 following the creation of Google DeepMind, making him one of the most influential figures shaping the trajectory of modern artificial intelligence.
