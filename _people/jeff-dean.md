Jeff Dean, born July 23, 1968, is a prominent American computer scientist and software engineer, renowned for his significant contributions to artificial intelligence and distributed systems. Since 2018, he has been the lead of Google AI, where he continues to innovate and drive the advancement of machine learning technologies.
