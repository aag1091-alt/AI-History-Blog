Jeffrey Adgate Dean, born July 23, 1968, is an American computer scientist and software engineer renowned for his pivotal role in advancing artificial intelligence and machine learning at Google. As the lead of Google AI and a key figure in the development of Google Brain, Dean has been instrumental in pushing the boundaries of neural network research and applications. His contributions extend beyond leadership, including significant scholarly work on neural computation and the mechanisms underlying intelligent systems.
