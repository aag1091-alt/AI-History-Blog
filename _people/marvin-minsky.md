Marvin Minsky, an influential American cognitive scientist and mathematician, was a pivotal figure in the development of artificial intelligence and cognitive science. A Harvard and Princeton graduate, Minsky joined MIT in 1958, where he co-founded the AI Laboratory and made groundbreaking contributions to the understanding of human thought and machine intelligence.
