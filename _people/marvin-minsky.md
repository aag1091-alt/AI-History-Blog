Marvin Minsky, a Harvard- and Princeton-trained mathematician, was a seminal figure in the development of artificial intelligence and cognitive science. A co-founder of MIT's AI laboratory, Minsky's work laid the foundational theories that shaped the trajectory of AI and his influential book 'The Society of Mind' explored the complexities of human thought and consciousness.
