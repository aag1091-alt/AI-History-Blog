Jakob Uszkoreit is a German machine learning researcher who contributed to the transformer architecture during his time at Google. After co-authoring the influential 2017 paper, he pivoted to computational biology and founded Inceptive, which uses AI to design novel RNA molecules for therapeutic applications.
