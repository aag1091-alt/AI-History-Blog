Jakob Uszkoreit is a machine learning researcher who co-authored the 2017 'Attention Is All You Need' paper at Google, one of the most influential publications in modern AI, which introduced the Transformer architecture underpinning models like GPT and BERT. After shaping the foundation of the generative AI revolution, he pivoted to the life sciences, co-founding Inceptive to harness deep learning for RNA drug design. His career arc—from redefining how machines process language to engineering molecules that could treat disease—exemplifies the broadening reach of AI research.
