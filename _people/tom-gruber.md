Tom Gruber, an American computer scientist born in 1959, is renowned for his pioneering work in ontology engineering and his role in defining ontologies in AI. A visionary entrepreneur, he co-founded Siri Inc., which revolutionized personal assistants with its intelligent navigator, acquired by Apple and now a cornerstone of iOS.
