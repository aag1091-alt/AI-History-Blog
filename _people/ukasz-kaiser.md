Łukasz Kaiser is a machine learning researcher whose co-authorship of the 2017 paper 'Attention Is All You Need' helped launch the Transformer architecture—now the engine behind virtually every large language model in existence. With roots in formal logic and automata theory, evidenced by his work 'Logic and Games on Automatic Structures,' Kaiser brings a rare blend of mathematical rigor and engineering ambition to the field of deep learning.
