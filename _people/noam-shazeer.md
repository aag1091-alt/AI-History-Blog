Noam Shazeer is an American computer scientist and entrepreneur whose work on the Transformer architecture fundamentally reshaped artificial intelligence and natural language processing. A driving force behind some of Google's most influential AI research, he co-authored the 'Attention Is All You Need' paper and continued advancing Transformer efficiency through innovations like GLU variants. He went on to co-found Character.AI, channeling decades of deep learning expertise into building next-generation conversational agents.
