Sir Demis Hassabis, born in 1976, is a British AI researcher and entrepreneur known for his pivotal role in advancing artificial intelligence. As the CEO and co-founder of Google DeepMind and Isomorphic Labs, he has revolutionized the field with innovations that extend from game-playing algorithms to complex protein structure prediction, earning him accolades such as the Nobel Prize in Chemistry and a knighthood for his groundbreaking work.
