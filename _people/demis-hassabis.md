Demis Hassabis, a British AI researcher and entrepreneur, is the co-founder and CEO of Google DeepMind, where he has spearheaded groundbreaking research in artificial intelligence. His work, which includes the development of innovative algorithms for protein structure prediction, has not only earned him scientific accolades but also a place among the most influential individuals shaping the future of technology.
