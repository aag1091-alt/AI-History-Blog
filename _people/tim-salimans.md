Tim Salimans is a machine-learning researcher who earned his Ph.D. from Erasmus University Rotterdam in 2013 and went on to shape the foundations of generative AI. At OpenAI he co-developed landmark techniques for training GANs and explored evolution strategies as a scalable alternative to reinforcement learning, before moving to Google Brain where he advanced diffusion-model efficiency. His methods are embedded in the infrastructure behind modern image-generation systems.
