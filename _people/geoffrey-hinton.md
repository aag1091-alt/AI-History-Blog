Geoffrey Hinton, born on December 6, 1947, is a renowned British-Canadian computer scientist and cognitive psychologist celebrated as 'the Godfather of AI'. His groundbreaking work on artificial neural networks, particularly the backpropagation algorithm, has been pivotal in advancing machine learning capabilities. At the University of Toronto and through his role at Google Brain, Hinton has continuously pushed the boundaries of AI research.
