Born April 22, 1985, Sam Altman dropped out of Stanford to co-found Loopt, then rose to president of Y Combinator before taking the helm at OpenAI in 2019. He oversaw the blockbuster launch of ChatGPT in 2022, making him one of the most influential figures in the AI revolution—though his brief ousting by OpenAI's board in 2023 underscored the high-stakes tensions at the heart of the industry.
