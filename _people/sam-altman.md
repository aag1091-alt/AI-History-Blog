Sam Altman, born in 1985, is an influential American entrepreneur and CEO of OpenAI since 2019, known for his pivotal role in launching ChatGPT and shaping the trajectory of the AI revolution. A Stanford dropout, he has a proven track record of success, notably through his venture Loopt and his leadership at Y Combinator.
