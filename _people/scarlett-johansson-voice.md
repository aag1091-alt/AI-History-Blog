Scarlett Johansson, renowned for her versatile acting career, has also lent her voice to the realm of artificial intelligence research. In a significant contribution, Johansson's voice became synonymous with an innovative approach to adaptive group testing, featured in the paper 'Noisy Adaptive Group Testing via Noisy Binary Search' on arXiv. This work aims to improve the efficiency of identifying defective items in large datasets, showcasing Johansson's unique impact beyond the film industry.
