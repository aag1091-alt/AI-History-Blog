Scarlett Johansson, beyond her renowned acting career, has left a mark in the annals of AI history. Her voice was integral in a key academic study on noisy adaptive group testing, which explores efficient strategies in noisy environments. Johansson also gained prominence in legal and ethical debates surrounding AI after she took OpenAI to court over the unauthorized use of her voice, raising critical questions about the ownership and exploitation of personal data in the digital age.
