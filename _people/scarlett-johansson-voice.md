Scarlett Johansson became an unlikely landmark figure in AI history after her captivating portrayal of the AI assistant Samantha in 'Her' envisioned a future where humans fall in love with machines. A decade later, she thrust the ethics of AI into the spotlight when she challenged OpenAI for releasing a synthetic voice she said mimicked her own without permission, forcing the industry to confront questions of consent, identity, and the boundaries of imitation in the age of generative AI.
