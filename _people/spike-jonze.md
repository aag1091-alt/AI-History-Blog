Spike Jonze, born Adam Spiegel in 1969, is a multi-talented American filmmaker, actor, musician, and photographer. Starting his career as a teenager photographing BMX riders and skateboarders for magazines, Jonze transitioned into filmmaking with skateboarding films and became an in-demand director of music videos, collaborating with numerous renowned artists. His diverse portfolio includes the innovative film 'Her', cementing his status as a visionary in both independent and mainstream cinema.
