Born Adam Spiegel on October 22, 1969, Spike Jonze is an American filmmaker, actor, musician, and photographer who rose from photographing BMX riders for Freestylin' Magazine as a teenager to directing some of the most inventive films and music videos of his generation. He is best known in AI circles for directing 'Her' (2013), a hauntingly plausible portrait of human-AI intimacy that became a cultural touchstone for debates about artificial intelligence, consciousness, and emotional connection. His career spans skateboard videos, commercials, and feature films including 'Being John Malkovich' and 'Where the Wild Things Are,' all united by a restless creative curiosity.
