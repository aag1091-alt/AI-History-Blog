Spike Jonze, born Adam Spiegel in 1969, is an American filmmaker, actor, musician, and photographer whose career has spanned a diverse range of media, from skateboarding films to music videos and feature films. He began his career photographing BMX and skateboarders for magazines, co-founding influential youth culture publications, and later became a sought-after director for music videos, collaborating with some of the most prominent bands and artists of the 1990s and 2000s.
