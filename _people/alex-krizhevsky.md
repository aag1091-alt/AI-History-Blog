Alex Krizhevsky is a Canadian computer scientist celebrated for his pivotal role in advancing deep learning through the creation of AlexNet, which demonstrated the power of GPU-accelerated neural networks for visual recognition tasks. His work with Geoffrey Hinton and Ilya Sutskever marked a turning point in AI research, setting the stage for modern deep learning applications.
