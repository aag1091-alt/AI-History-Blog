Illia Polosukhin is a Ukrainian-born computer scientist and entrepreneur who co-authored the transformer paper at Google. After leaving Google, he co-founded NEAR Protocol, a sharded proof-of-stake blockchain designed for ease of use and developer accessibility. He is notable for bridging the worlds of cutting-edge AI research and decentralized technology.
