Illia Polosukhin is a Ukrainian-born computer scientist and entrepreneur who helped reshape both artificial intelligence and blockchain technology. As a co-author of the transformer architecture—the foundation behind large language models like GPT and Claude—he left an indelible mark on modern AI, then channeled that expertise into co-founding NEAR Protocol, one of the most developer-friendly layer-1 blockchains. His work sits at the rare intersection of foundational AI research and decentralized systems, bridging two of the most consequential technologies of the era.
