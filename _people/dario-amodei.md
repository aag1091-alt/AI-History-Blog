Dario Amodei (born 1983) is an American AI researcher and entrepreneur who co-founded Anthropic in 2021 after serving as Vice President of Research at OpenAI. A Princeton Ph.D., he steers the development of Anthropic's Claude model series while advocating for an 'entente' strategy in which democratic nations harness advanced AI to secure a decisive edge and share its benefits with cooperating allies.
