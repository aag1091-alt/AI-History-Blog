Dario Amodei, born in 1983, is a leading American AI researcher and entrepreneur, known for his pivotal role in shaping the ethical landscape of AI technology. As co-founder and CEO of Anthropic, Amodei champions the development of AI systems that prioritize safety and societal benefit, while also advocating for an 'entente' approach to international AI collaboration and security.
