Brando Benifei, born on January 1, 1986, in La Spezia, Italy, is a prominent Italian politician who began his political career with the Democrats of the Left in 2002 and later joined the Democratic Party in 2007. He has been a Member of the European Parliament since 2014, focusing on European policies and legislative efforts that impact the continent.
