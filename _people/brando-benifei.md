Brando Benifei, born January 1, 1986, in La Spezia, Italy, is a prominent Italian politician who has been a member of the European Parliament since 2014. An advocate for progressive policies, he has been re-elected twice, solidifying his role as a key figure in EU politics. His work extends beyond legislative duties to include research on migration policies and EU governance.
