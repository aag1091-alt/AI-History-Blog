Isaac Asimov, born in 1920 and passing away in 1992, was an American writer and biochemist celebrated for his immense literary output and pioneering ideas in science fiction. A prolific author of over 500 books, Asimov's works spanned numerous genres, with his hard science fiction, the Foundation and Robot series, cementing his legacy as a visionary in the field.
