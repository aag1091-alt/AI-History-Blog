Isaac Asimov was a renowned American author, biochemist, and computer scientist. He is best known for his science fiction works, including the Foundation series, as well as his popular non-fiction book, 'The Intelligent Man's Guide to Science'. His groundbreaking research in robotics and artificial intelligence led to numerous breakthroughs in fields like computer science and engineering.
