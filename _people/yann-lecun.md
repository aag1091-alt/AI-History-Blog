Yann LeCun, a French–American computer scientist, is renowned for his groundbreaking work in artificial intelligence, particularly in the realm of convolutional neural networks (CNNs), which have transformed computer vision and pattern recognition. As the Jacob T. Schwartz Professor at New York University and a former Chief AI Scientist at Meta Platforms, LeCun continues to push the boundaries of autonomous machine intelligence, emphasizing the development of reliable world models and complex reasoning capabilities in AI systems.
