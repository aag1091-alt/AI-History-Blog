Yann LeCun, a French–American computer scientist, is celebrated for his groundbreaking contributions to artificial intelligence and machine learning, particularly in the realm of convolutional neural networks. As the Jacob T. Schwartz Professor at New York University, he has significantly advanced the fields of computer vision and robotics, and his work on DjVu stands out as a pioneering achievement in image compression technology.
