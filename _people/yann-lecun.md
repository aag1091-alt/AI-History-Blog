Yann LeCun is a French-American computer scientist whose pioneering work on convolutional neural networks laid the foundation for modern computer vision and deep learning. As the Jacob T. Schwartz Professor at NYU's Courant Institute and former Chief AI Scientist at Meta, he has shaped both the theory and industrial application of AI. His 2018 Turing Award, shared with Bengio and Hinton, cemented his status as one of the architects of the deep learning revolution.
