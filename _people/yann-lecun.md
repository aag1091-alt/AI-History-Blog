Yann LeCun is a renowned computer scientist and AI researcher. He is credited with pioneering work on Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) that revolutionized image recognition tasks in 1998, with his paper introducing this fundamental concept. Throughout his career, he has made significant contributions to the development of artificial neural networks, most notably through his work on LeNet-1 and LeNet-5.
