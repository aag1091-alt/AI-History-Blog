Jia Deng is a computer scientist who played a pivotal role in building ImageNet, the landmark dataset of millions of labeled images that became the proving ground for modern deep learning. A Tsinghua University graduate who earned his Ph.D. in 2012, he helped design the ImageNet challenge that sparked the deep learning revolution when deep neural networks first dominated the competition. His work on large-scale visual recognition and structured knowledge continues to shape how machines understand the visual world.
