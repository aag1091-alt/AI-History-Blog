David Silver, born in 1976, is a prominent computer scientist and AI researcher at DeepMind and University College London. He is renowned for his work on reinforcement learning, particularly the development of AlphaZero and MuZero, which have significantly advanced the capabilities of AI in strategic games and beyond.
