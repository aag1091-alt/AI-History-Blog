David Silver, born in 1976, is a renowned computer scientist and AI researcher known for his pioneering work at DeepMind. He gained international recognition for developing AlphaZero, a system that showcases the potential of self-play in mastering complex tasks like chess and Go. Currently, he continues to push the boundaries of AI with projects such as the Gemini 2.5 Pro model, while also teaching at University College London.
