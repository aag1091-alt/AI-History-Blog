Brad Rutter is an American game show legend who dominated Jeopardy! like few others, retiring undefeated from his original 2000 run and then winning every tournament he entered for nearly 20 years. With over $5.1 million in Jeopardy! earnings alone, he remains the highest-earning contestant in the show's syndicated history. His remarkable streak of never losing to a human opponent finally ended at the 2020 GOAT Tournament, cementing his place among the greatest quiz competitors of all time.
