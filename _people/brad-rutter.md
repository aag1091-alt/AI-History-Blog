Brad Rutter, born Bradford Gates Rutter on January 31, 1978, is a legendary American game show contestant who has redefined success on Jeopardy!, amassing over $5 million in winnings. His dominance on the show was unbroken until 2020, making him a standout figure in the realm of game show competition. Beyond his game show achievements, Rutter has expanded his influence in entertainment by hosting, producing, and acting, cementing his legacy as a multi-faceted talent.
