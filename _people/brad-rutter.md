Brad Rutter, born in 1978, is an American game show icon, renowned for his unparalleled success on Jeopardy!, where he has amassed over $5.1 million in winnings, making him the third-highest-earning contestant in American game show history. His legacy includes an undefeated streak of 22 matches, only interrupted by the 2020 GOAT Tournament, where he finished third.
