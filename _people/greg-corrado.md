Greg Corrado is an American computer scientist and neuroscientist renowned for his pivotal role in the development of Google Brain. As a Distinguished Scientist at Google, he has been instrumental in leading the application of artificial intelligence in healthcare and advancing the frontiers of machine learning and computational neuroscience.
