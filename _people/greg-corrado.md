Greg Corrado, an American machine learning scientist, is renowned for co-founding Google Brain, a pioneering initiative that integrated deep learning into Google's technological arsenal. As a Distinguished Scientist at Google, he also directed the Health AI division, focusing on the practical application of AI in healthcare.
