Josef 'Sepp' Hochreiter, a German computer scientist born in 1967, has made landmark contributions to artificial intelligence, particularly through the development of the LSTM neural network, which addresses the vanishing gradient problem in recurrent neural networks. As a professor at the Johannes Kepler University of Linz, Hochreiter has also led significant research institutions and conferences, cementing his role as a pivotal figure in advancing the fields of machine learning and bioinformatics.
