Born Andrew Wachowski on December 28, 1967, Lana Wachowski is an American filmmaker known for her innovative storytelling style. She has been recognized with numerous awards for her work, including two Academy Award nominations. In 2022, she became the first non-binary person to be included in Time Magazine's 100 most influential people in the world.
