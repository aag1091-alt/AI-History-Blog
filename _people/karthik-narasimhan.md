Karthik Narasimhan earned his Ph.D. from MIT in 2017 and joined Princeton University's computer science faculty, where he leads research at the intersection of natural language processing and reinforcement learning. His work on training agents to understand and act on language—from text-adventure games to real-world software engineering—has shaped the modern vision of AI systems that don't just generate text but take meaningful action in complex environments.
