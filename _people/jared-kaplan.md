Jared Kaplan is a theoretical physicist turned AI researcher whose work on neural scaling laws reshaped how the field understands large language models. He co-founded Anthropic, where he serves as Chief Science Officer, channeling his physics-trained intuition into building safer AI systems. He holds a faculty position in the Johns Hopkins University Department of Physics & Astronomy.
