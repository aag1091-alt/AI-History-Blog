Tom Brown is a data scientist who became one of the most influential figures in modern AI as the lead author of the GPT-3 paper at OpenAI. His 2020 work showed that scaling language models to 175 billion parameters unlocked remarkable few-shot learning abilities, igniting the large language model revolution that transformed the technology industry.
