Jürgen Schmidhuber, born in 1963, is a German computer scientist and artificial intelligence researcher renowned for his pioneering work on LSTM neural networks. His research also delves into the principles of curiosity and creativity, contributing to a deeper understanding of how machines and humans perceive interest and beauty in data and patterns.
