Joaquin Rafael Phoenix, born October 28, 1974, is an American actor renowned for his raw and complex performances in independent films. Starting his career as a child actor alongside his brother River, Phoenix later gained critical acclaim for his unconventional roles, including his Oscar-winning portrayal in 'Joker'. His career is marked by a series of accolades that cement his status as one of the most acclaimed actors of his generation.
