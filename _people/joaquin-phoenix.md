Born Joaquin Rafael Bottom on October 28, 1974, Joaquin Phoenix began acting in television alongside his brother River in the early 1980s, initially crediting himself as Leaf Phoenix. Widely regarded as one of the preeminent actors of his generation, he has built a career defined by fearless performances in independent and mainstream film, earning an Academy Award, a Grammy, a BAFTA, and two Golden Globes among numerous other accolades.
