James Cameron transformed cinema by relentlessly pushing the boundaries of what computers could render on screen, from the shape-shifting T-1000 to the fully realized alien world of Pandora. His obsession with merging storytelling and cutting-edge technology drove massive leaps in CGI, motion capture, and AI-assisted visual effects, making him one of the most influential figures in the intersection of artificial intelligence and entertainment. Every blockbuster that uses digital humans or virtual production owes a debt to Cameron's audacious bets on technology that didn't yet exist.
