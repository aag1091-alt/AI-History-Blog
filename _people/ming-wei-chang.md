Ming-Wei Chang is a computer scientist whose career spans top-tier academia and industry, from National Taiwan University to a Ph.D. at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2012, and onward to research roles at Microsoft and Google. His work has advanced the frontiers of natural language processing and machine learning, contributing to systems that help machines understand and generate human language at scale.
