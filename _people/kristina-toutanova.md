Kristina Toutanova is a researcher whose work on natural language processing has shaped the modern AI landscape. She is best known as a co-author of BERT, the deep bidirectional transformer model that redefined how machines understand language, and she continues to push the frontier of efficient large-scale model training through innovations in pre-training data optimization.
