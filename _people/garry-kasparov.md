Garry Kimovich Kasparov, born Garik Weinstein in 1963, is a Russian chess grandmaster and political activist. He reigned as the World Chess Champion from 1985 to 2000, setting unprecedented records in chess rankings and tournament victories, while also making significant contributions to the field through his writings and activism.
