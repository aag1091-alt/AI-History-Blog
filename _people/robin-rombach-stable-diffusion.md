Robin Rombach is a German AI researcher who co-created Stable Diffusion while working at the CompVis group at Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich. His key insight—performing diffusion in a learned latent space instead of directly on pixels—made high-resolution image generation fast and accessible on consumer hardware. By releasing Stable Diffusion openly, Rombach helped ignite a revolution in AI-powered art and creativity that put generative AI into the hands of millions.
