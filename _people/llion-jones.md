Llion Jones is a Welsh computer scientist who was a researcher at Google and a co-author of the transformer paper. He remained at Google longer than most of his transformer co-authors, continuing work on AI research. He later founded Sakana AI, a Tokyo-based startup exploring nature-inspired approaches to artificial intelligence.
