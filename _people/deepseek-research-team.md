DeepSeek is a Chinese AI research lab, backed by the quantitative hedge fund High-Flyer, that stunned the global AI community by producing open-weight models competitive with the best from OpenAI and Google at dramatically lower cost. Their V3 and R1 model releases in late 2024 and early 2025 ignited a wave of innovation around efficient deployment and quantization, reshaping assumptions about what well-funded but lean teams could achieve in frontier AI research.
