Alexander Medawar Garland (born 26 May 1970) is an English author, screenwriter, and filmmaker who burst onto the literary scene with The Beach and pivoted to cinema with a string of genre-defining sci-fi scripts. His directorial debut Ex Machina became a landmark AI thriller, earning him an Oscar nomination and cementing his reputation as one of the most intellectually ambitious storytellers working in film today.
