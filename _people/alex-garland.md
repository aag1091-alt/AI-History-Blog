Alexander Medawar Garland, born on May 26, 1970, is a British author, screenwriter, and film director renowned for his unique blend of literary and cinematic prowess. He achieved critical acclaim with his debut novel 'The Beach' and furthered his reputation through acclaimed screenplays and directing the sci-fi thriller 'Ex Machina', which earned him an Academy Award nomination.
