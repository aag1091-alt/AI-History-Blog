Ashish Vaswani is an Indian computer scientist whose work at Google Brain fundamentally changed artificial intelligence. As a co-author of the 2017 paper 'Attention Is All You Need', he helped introduce the Transformer architecture — a design so powerful it became the engine behind BERT, ChatGPT, and virtually every major language model that followed. Previously affiliated with the University of Southern California's Information Sciences Institute, Vaswani's contribution stands as one of the most consequential breakthroughs in modern machine learning.
