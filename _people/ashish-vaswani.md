Ashish Vaswani (born 1986) is an Indian computer scientist who was a key researcher at Google Brain. He is best known as the lead author of "Attention Is All You Need," the 2017 paper that introduced the transformer architecture, which became the foundation for modern large language models. After leaving Google, he co-founded the AI startup Adept AI.
