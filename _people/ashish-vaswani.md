Ashish Vaswani, born in 1986, is a pioneering machine learning researcher known for his groundbreaking work on the Transformer model, a cornerstone of modern natural language processing. His contributions have been pivotal in advancing the field of AI, including influential work at both the Information Sciences Institute and Google Brain.
