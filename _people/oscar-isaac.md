Born in Guatemala in 1979 and raised in the United States, Oscar Isaac rose from punk band teenager and Juilliard graduate to one of Hollywood's most celebrated actors. Vanity Fair named him the best actor of his generation in 2017, and he has been widely credited with shattering stereotypes about Latino characters on screen. His accolades include a Golden Globe Award and recognition among the 25 greatest actors of the 21st century by The New York Times.
