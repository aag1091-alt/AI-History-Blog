Óscar Isaac Hernández Estrada, born in Guatemala and raised in the United States, is an acclaimed American actor known for his versatility and ability to challenge stereotypes. After graduating from the Juilliard School, Isaac rose to prominence with a series of character roles before landing his breakthrough performance in 'The Nativity Story' in 2006.
