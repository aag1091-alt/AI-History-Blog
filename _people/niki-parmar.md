Niki Parmar is an artificial intelligence researcher celebrated for her pioneering work in the development of the Image Transformer, a groundbreaking method for visual representation learning that has significantly advanced the field. Her research also includes the introduction of CountGD++, a novel approach to object counting in images and videos, addressing limitations in existing methods and demonstrating her commitment to pushing the boundaries of computer vision and AI.
