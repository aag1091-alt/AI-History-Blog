Ken Jennings, born on May 23, 1974, is an American game show contestant and author who achieved legendary status by winning 74 consecutive games on Jeopardy!, a record that stood for over two decades. Post his triumph, Jennings shifted focus to writing, penning several best-selling books that delve into American trivia and cultural history, while also appearing on and hosting other game shows.
