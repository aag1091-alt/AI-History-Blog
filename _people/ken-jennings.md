Kenneth Wayne Jennings III (born May 23, 1974) is a former software engineer who became a household name by winning 74 consecutive Jeopardy! games — the longest streak ever. A best-selling author and podcaster nicknamed 'The Professor,' he came full circle when he took over as host of the iconic quiz show in 2021.
