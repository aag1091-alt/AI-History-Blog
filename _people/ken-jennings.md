Ken Jennings, born May 23, 1974, is an American game show icon and author, best known for his unprecedented 74 consecutive wins on Jeopardy! and as the highest-earning contestant for over 20 years. After his remarkable run on the show, Jennings pivoted to a career as an author, exploring the realm of trivia and American culture in a series of best-selling books.
