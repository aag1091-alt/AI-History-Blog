Born in 1986, Ilya Sutskever is an Israeli-Canadian computer scientist whose work on AlexNet, sequence-to-sequence learning, and GPT models helped reshape artificial intelligence from a niche pursuit into a world-altering force. As co-founder and chief scientist of OpenAI, he guided the research that produced ChatGPT and pioneered reasoning models, earning him recognition as one of the most highly cited computer scientists in history.
