Ilya Sutskever, an Israeli-Canadian computer scientist, has been at the forefront of deep learning and AI research since the late 2000s. Notably, he co-founded and served as chief scientist at OpenAI, where his leadership contributed to the creation of influential models like GPT and the development of ChatGPT. His work spans significant contributions to convolutional neural networks, reasoning models, and embedding-based collaborative filtering.
