Elon Musk, born in 1971 in Pretoria, South Africa, is a visionary entrepreneur celebrated for his transformative roles at Tesla and SpaceX. With a net worth of around US$852 billion as of 2026, Musk has been the world's wealthiest person since 2025, owing much of his success to pioneering ventures in electric cars and space travel.
