Charles Babbage (1791-1871), an English polymath, is celebrated as the visionary behind the digital programmable computer. His conceptualization of the difference engine and the more sophisticated analytical engine laid the groundwork for modern computing. Beyond his groundbreaking work in computing, Babbage was also influential in the fields of manufacturing and philosophy, leaving a lasting impact on technology and society.
