Joseph Weizenbaum was a German-American computer scientist and professor at MIT, renowned for his contributions to the field of artificial intelligence and his critical stance on the ethical implications of technology. His creation of the ELIZA program and the publication of 'Computer Power and Human Reason' highlight his role in shaping the discourse around the impact of computing on human society.
