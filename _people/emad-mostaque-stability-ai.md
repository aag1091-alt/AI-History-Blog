Emad Mostaque is a British entrepreneur and former hedge fund analyst who founded Stability AI and brought Stable Diffusion to the world in 2022, igniting a global wave of open-source generative image creation. His advocacy for freely available AI models challenged the closed-source dominance of companies like OpenAI and Midjourney, though Stability AI faced financial turbulence and copyright controversies. He departed as CEO in 2024 to focus on decentralized AI efforts.
