Samuel Butler (1835–1902) was an English novelist and critic renowned for his satirical and thought-provoking works. His novel 'Erewhon' is a seminal piece that challenges Victorian society's views on technology and morality. Additionally, 'The Way of All Flesh' offers a candid and unconventional autobiography, delving into the complexities of human nature and societal norms.
