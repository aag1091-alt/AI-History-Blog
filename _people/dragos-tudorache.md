Dragoș Tudorache, born on January 14, 1975, is a prominent Romanian politician and technocrat, currently serving as a Member of the European Parliament where he champions digital and economic policies. His career spans significant contributions in both political and academic spheres, including pioneering work in data compression and a leadership role within the National Liberal Party.
