Born in China in 1976, Fei-Fei Li is a Stanford computer scientist whose creation of ImageNet ignited the deep learning revolution in computer vision. She co-directs the Stanford Institute for Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence and co-founded AI4ALL to broaden access to the field. Named one of Time's 100 Most Influential People in AI in 2023, Li has shaped both the technical foundations and the social conscience of modern artificial intelligence.
