Fei-Fei Li, a pioneering computer scientist, is renowned for founding ImageNet, a dataset pivotal in the rapid progress of computer vision technology. As a professor at Stanford University and co-director of the Stanford Institute for Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence, she continues to lead transformative research in AI and machine learning, advocating for ethical and inclusive practices in technology.
