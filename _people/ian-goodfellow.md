Ian J. Goodfellow, born in 1987, is an American computer scientist and engineer renowned for his work in artificial neural networks and deep learning. His invention of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) has been a game-changer in the field, and he is also known for co-authoring the influential textbook 'Deep Learning', which has been adopted by over 1,500 universities globally.
