Ian Goodfellow (born 1987) is an American computer scientist who fundamentally changed the landscape of generative AI by inventing GANs in 2014, giving machines the ability to create strikingly realistic images, videos, and data. He has held research roles at Google Brain, OpenAI, and Apple before joining Google DeepMind, and co-authored the definitive textbook 'Deep Learning' that trained a generation of practitioners in the field.
