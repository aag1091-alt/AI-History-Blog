Frank Rosenblatt was an American psychologist who made significant contributions to the field of artificial intelligence. He is credited with developing the first perceptron, a type of artificial neural network, and pioneered the use of affine invariant quantities in optimization algorithms. His work had a lasting impact on the development of machine learning and deep learning.
