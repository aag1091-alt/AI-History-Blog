Frank Rosenblatt, an American psychologist born in 1928, is celebrated as a pioneer in artificial intelligence. His seminal work on the Perceptron model in the 1950s advanced the concept of artificial neural networks, earning him the moniker 'Father of Deep Learning'. Despite challenges and skepticism, Rosenblatt's research significantly propelled the field towards contemporary machine learning practices.
