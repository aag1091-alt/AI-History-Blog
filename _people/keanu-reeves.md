Keanu Charles Reeves, born in Beirut and raised in Toronto, is a Canadian actor and musician celebrated for his breakthrough roles in Bill & Ted's Excellent Adventure and My Own Private Idaho, and his iconic performances in The Matrix and John Wick franchises. With a career spanning over four decades, Reeves is known for his amiable public image and philanthropic efforts.
