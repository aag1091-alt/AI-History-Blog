Keanu Charles Reeves, born in 1964 in Beirut and raised in Toronto, is a versatile Canadian actor and musician. His career, spanning four decades, is marked by leading roles in action films and a reputation for philanthropy and amiable public image. Reeves' breakthrough roles in the 'Bill & Ted' series and his acclaimed performance in 'My Own Private Idaho' set the stage for his continued success and recognition.
