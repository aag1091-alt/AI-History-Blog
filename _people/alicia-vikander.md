Alicia Vikander, a Swedish actress born in 1988, began her acting career in minor stage productions at the Gothenburg Opera House and later trained as a ballet dancer in Stockholm. She gained international recognition for her role in the period drama 'Anna Karenina' and further solidified her status as a leading actress with her Academy Award-winning performance in 'The Danish Girl'.
