Born in Gothenburg, Sweden in 1988, Alicia Vikander trained as a ballet dancer at the Royal Swedish Ballet before captivating audiences on screen. She rose to international prominence with roles in Anna Karenina and A Royal Affair, and won an Academy Award and Screen Actors Guild Award over the course of her career. Her portrayal of the humanoid AI Ava in Ex Machina (2015) made her an iconic figure at the intersection of cinema and artificial intelligence storytelling.
