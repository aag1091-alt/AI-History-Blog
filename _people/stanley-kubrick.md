Stanley Kubrick (1928–1999) was an American filmmaker and photographer renowned for his meticulous approach to filmmaking and groundbreaking visual style. After early work as a photographer, he directed critically acclaimed films such as 'Paths of Glory' and 'Lolita', before venturing into ambitious projects like '2001: A Space Odyssey' and 'The Shining', which continue to influence filmmakers worldwide.
