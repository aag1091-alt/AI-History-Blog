Stanley Kubrick, an American filmmaker and photographer born in 1928, emerged from a background in photography to become a major figure in post-war cinema. His films, known for their intricate detail and dark humor, include classics like '2001: A Space Odyssey' and 'The Shining', solidifying his legacy as one of the most influential directors in film history.
