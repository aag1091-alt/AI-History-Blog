Greg Brockman is an American entrepreneur and software engineer who left MIT in 2010 to join Stripe, rising to CTO by age 25. In 2015 he co-founded OpenAI, where he has served as both CTO and president, playing a central role in building the organization behind some of the most transformative AI systems ever created.
