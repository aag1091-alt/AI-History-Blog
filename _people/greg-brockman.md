Greg Brockman, born in 1987, is an influential entrepreneur and software engineer known for his pivotal role in founding and leading OpenAI. Prior to OpenAI, he was instrumental in Stripe's rapid growth and success as the company's CTO. Brockman's expertise and vision continue to shape the landscape of artificial intelligence and technology entrepreneurship.
