Sir Ridley Scott, born in 1937, is an influential English filmmaker renowned for his atmospheric and visually stunning films. After a successful career in television and advertising, Scott transitioned to film direction with 'The Duellists' in 1977, but it was 'Alien' that solidified his place in the industry. His subsequent works, spanning diverse genres from science fiction to historical epics, have earned him critical acclaim and numerous accolades.
