John Michael Jumper is an American chemist and AI expert who led the team behind AlphaFold2, the deep-learning system that cracked one of biology's grandest challenges: predicting a protein's 3D structure from its amino acid sequence. His work earned him the 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry and unleashed a wave of breakthroughs in drug discovery, enzyme engineering, and our fundamental understanding of life's molecular machinery.
