Daniela Amodei, born in 1987, is an influential American entrepreneur and AI researcher known for co-founding Anthropic, a company dedicated to creating ethical and safe AI. Her work encompasses not only the development of sophisticated AI systems but also the philosophical exploration of machine learning ethics.
