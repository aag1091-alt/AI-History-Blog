Born July 30, 1947, in Austria, Arnold Schwarzenegger began lifting weights at 15 and won Mr. Universe at just 20, going on to claim seven Mr. Olympia titles and earn the nickname 'Austrian Oak.' He parlayed his bodybuilding fame into a massive Hollywood career and later entered politics, serving as California's 38th governor from 2003 to 2011.
