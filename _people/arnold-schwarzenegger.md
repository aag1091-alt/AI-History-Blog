Arnold Alois Schwarzenegger, born July 30, 1947, is a former bodybuilder turned Hollywood superstar and politician. Renowned for his bulging muscles and booming voice, Schwarzenegger rose to international fame through his unparalleled success in bodybuilding and his starring roles in action movies. His political career included a notable tenure as the Governor of California, where he championed environmental and healthcare reforms.
