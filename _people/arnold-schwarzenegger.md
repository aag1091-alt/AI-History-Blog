Arnold Alois Schwarzenegger, born July 30, 1947, is a multifaceted icon known for his groundbreaking roles in action films and his legendary career in bodybuilding. From lifting weights at 15 and winning Mr. Universe at 20, to his political journey as California's governor, Schwarzenegger has left an indelible mark on both the entertainment and political landscapes.
