Thierry Breton, born January 15, 1955, is a French business executive and politician celebrated for his influential roles in both the private and public sectors. His career spans leadership roles at major corporations like Thomson-RCA and France Télécom, and a significant stint as European Commissioner for Internal Market under Ursula von der Leyen, where he championed technology and market regulations. Breton's ability to navigate the complexities of business and politics has made him a key figure in European economic policy.
