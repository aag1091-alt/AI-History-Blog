Thierry Breton, born in 1955, is a prominent French business executive and politician renowned for his leadership roles in major French technology companies and his influential career in government. From his tenure as CEO of Groupe Bull and France Télécom to his roles in the French government and as European Commissioner for Internal Market, Breton has significantly shaped the European tech and telecom landscapes.
