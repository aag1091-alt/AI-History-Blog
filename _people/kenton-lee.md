Kenton Lee is a distinguished researcher known for his contributions to both botany and natural language processing. His work includes a comprehensive study on Microseris, a genus of flowering plants, and significant research efforts at Google. Lee is also recognized for his technical contributions to the development of HyperCLOVA X, a state-of-the-art language model tailored to the nuances of the Korean language and culture.
