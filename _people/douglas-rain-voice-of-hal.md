Canadian actor Douglas Rain is renowned for his role as the voice of HAL 9000, a pivotal AI in '2000: A Space Odyssey'. His contribution to cinema has transcended art and science, with the HAL QCD method named in his honor, a testament to the interdisciplinary influence of his work.
