Douglas Rain, born on September 3, 1924, is best known for voicing HAL 9000 in Stanley Kubrick's 1968 film 2001: A Space Odyssey. His portrayal of the sentient computer remains a defining moment in cinema history, influencing not only pop culture but also the scientific community, as exemplified by the naming of the HAL QCD Collaboration in honor of his work.
