Lilly Wachowski (born December 29, 1967) is a Polish-American film director, writer, and producer who, together with her sister Lana, revolutionized science fiction cinema as the Wachowskis. The duo made their directing debut with Bound (1996) before achieving global fame with The Matrix trilogy, blending philosophy, martial arts, and cyberpunk into a cultural phenomenon. A trans woman and influential creative force, she has continued to push boundaries in both storytelling and representation in Hollywood.
