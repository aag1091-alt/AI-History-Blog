Lilly Wachowski, born December 29, 1967, is an American film and television director, writer, and producer. Along with her sister Lana, Lilly co-founded the Wachowskis, a directing duo renowned for their influential contributions to science fiction and cyberpunk genres. Their collaboration includes the iconic Matrix trilogy and the graphic novel adaptation V for Vendetta, both of which have left indelible marks on popular culture.
