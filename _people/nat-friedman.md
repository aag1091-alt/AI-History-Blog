Nat Friedman is an American technology executive who rose to prominence leading GitHub as CEO, where he oversaw the integration of AI into everyday software development. A longtime open-source advocate and former chairman of the GNOME Foundation, he now drives product strategy at Meta Superintelligence Labs while serving on the board of the Arc Institute and advising Midjourney.
