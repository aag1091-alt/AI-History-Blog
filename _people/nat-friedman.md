Nat Friedman, an American technology executive and investor, has made significant contributions to both open-source software and artificial intelligence governance. His tenure as CEO of GitHub was marked by substantial growth and innovation in software development practices. He also played a crucial role in the GNOME Foundation, enhancing the Linux desktop environment. Currently, Friedman leads product initiatives at Meta Superintelligence Labs, where he focuses on the development of ethical AI technologies.
