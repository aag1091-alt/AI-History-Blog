Aidan Gomez is a British-Canadian computer scientist who co-authored the transformer paper during an internship at Google Brain while studying at the University of Toronto. He co-founded and serves as CEO of Cohere, a company that provides enterprise large language model solutions, making him one of the most prominent young leaders in the AI industry.
