Lee Sedol, a 9 dan professional Go player from South Korea, achieved unprecedented success in international titles and was known as 'The Strong Stone' for his formidable play style. His historic match against AlphaGo in 2016 marked a pivotal moment in the evolution of the game, leading him to ultimately retire due to the increasing dominance of artificial intelligence.
