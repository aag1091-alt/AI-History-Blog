Lee Sedol, born on March 2, 1983, is a South Korean former professional Go player renowned for his exceptional skill and the historic matches he played against the AI program AlphaGo. Known as 'The Strong Stone', Sedol's legacy is cemented by his significant achievements in Go and his influential role in demonstrating the growing power of artificial intelligence.
