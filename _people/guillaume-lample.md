Guillaume Lample is a French AI researcher who reshaped the field of natural language processing with his work on unsupervised machine translation and cross-lingual pretraining at Facebook AI Research. After co-authoring influential papers—including work showing neural networks can perform symbolic mathematics—and contributing to Meta's Llama project, he co-founded Mistral AI, which quickly rose to become Europe's most prominent open-weight AI company.
