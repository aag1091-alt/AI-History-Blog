Guillaume Lample, born in 1984, is a multifaceted Canadian artist and researcher. As a filmmaker and novelist, he has made notable contributions to Quebec's television and film industries with works like 'Adulthood' and 'Niagara'. Simultaneously, he has advanced the field of artificial intelligence by demonstrating the potential of deep learning in symbolic mathematics, contributing to the paper 'Deep Learning for Symbolic Mathematics'.
