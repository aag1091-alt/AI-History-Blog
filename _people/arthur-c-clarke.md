Sir Arthur C. Clarke was an English science fiction writer, science writer, and futurist whose visionary works and pioneering efforts in space advocacy earned him the title 'Prophet of the Space Age'. A prolific author and recipient of the Kalinga Prize, Clarke's writings, including the screenplay for 2001: A Space Odyssey, have profoundly influenced the public's perception of space exploration and futuristic technology.
