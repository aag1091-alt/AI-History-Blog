Sir Arthur C. Clarke was an English science fiction writer, science writer, futurist, and inventor whose visionary works foresaw the practical use of satellites for communications and inspired generations of scientists and engineers. His writings and popular science work earned him a global following and a reputation as a leading authority on space exploration.
