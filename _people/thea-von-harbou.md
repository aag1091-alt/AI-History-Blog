Thea Gabriele von Harbou (1888-1954) was a German screenwriter, novelist, film director, and actress, best known for her collaboration with Fritz Lang on the science fiction masterpiece Metropolis. Her multifaceted career in the German film industry during the 1920s and 1930s saw her not only writing influential scripts but also directing and acting, making her a versatile and significant figure in early cinema.
