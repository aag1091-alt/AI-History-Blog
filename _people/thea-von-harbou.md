Thea von Harbou (1888–1954) was a versatile German artist known for her contributions to early cinema as a screenwriter, novelist, director, and actress. She is celebrated for her novel and screenplay for the 1927 silent film Metropolis, a landmark in science fiction and cinematic artistry.
