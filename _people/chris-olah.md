Chris Olah (born c. 1993) is a machine learning researcher who has become one of the foremost voices in understanding what happens inside neural networks. His groundbreaking work on mechanistic interpretability and neural network visualization has shaped how the field thinks about AI transparency. He co-founded Anthropic, channeling his deep commitment to understanding AI systems into building safer ones.
