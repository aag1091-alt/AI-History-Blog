William L. 'Red' Whittaker (born 1948) is an American roboticist and Fredkin Research Professor at Carnegie Mellon University's Robotics Institute, where he also directs the Field Robotics Center. He led Tartan Racing to victory in the 2007 DARPA Urban Challenge and has spent decades pushing the boundaries of autonomous vehicles, planetary rovers, and field robotics.
