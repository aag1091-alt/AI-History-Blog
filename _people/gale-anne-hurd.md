Gale Anne Hurd, born on October 25, 1955, is a pioneering American film and television producer known for her seminal work in the science fiction and drama genres. As the founder of Valhalla Entertainment, she has been instrumental in bringing to life such groundbreaking works as 'The Terminator,' 'Aliens,' 'The Abyss,' and 'The Walking Dead.'
