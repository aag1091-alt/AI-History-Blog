Gale Anne Hurd is an American film and television producer who helped define modern sci-fi and action cinema through landmark productions like The Terminator, Aliens, and Armageddon. As the founder of Valhalla Entertainment and a former recording secretary for the Producers Guild of America, she has shaped the industry both on screen and behind the scenes. Her work spans decades, from 1980s blockbusters to the cultural phenomenon of The Walking Dead.
