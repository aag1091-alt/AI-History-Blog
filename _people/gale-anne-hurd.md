Gale Anne Hurd, born on October 25, 1955, is an American film and television producer renowned for her impactful contributions to science fiction and horror genres. Her career highlights include producing seminal films such as 'The Terminator' and 'Aliens,' and her television work on 'The Walking Dead,' which earned her a place as one of the most influential producers in the industry.
