Adam Cheyer, born around 1966, is an American computer scientist renowned for co-founding Siri Inc., a pioneering company in conversational AI technology. His work at Apple further solidified his impact on the field, directing the engineering of Siri into the iPhone. Beyond his industry contributions, Cheyer has made notable academic contributions, particularly with 'The Open Agent Architecture,' a seminal paper that laid the groundwork for agent-based software systems.
