Sir Michael James Lighthill (1924-1998) was a British applied mathematician renowned for his pioneering work in aeroacoustics and his critical assessment of artificial intelligence. His influential Lighthill report in 1973 had a profound impact on the AI research community, leading to a period of reduced funding and interest in the field.
