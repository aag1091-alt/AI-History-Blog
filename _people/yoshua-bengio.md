Born March 5, 1964, Yoshua Bengio is a Canadian computer scientist and professor at the Université de Montréal who helped ignite the deep learning revolution. One of three researchers known as the 'Godfathers of AI,' he co-founded the field's modern foundations and built Mila into one of the world's largest academic AI research labs. He is the most-cited living scientist across all fields by total citations.
