Yoshua Bengio, born March 5, 1964, is a pioneering Canadian computer scientist and professor at Université de Montréal, renowned for his foundational contributions to artificial neural networks and deep learning. His work has been instrumental in transforming AI from theoretical concepts to practical applications, earning him the prestigious ACM A.M. Turing Award alongside fellow AI trailblazers Geoffrey Hinton and Yann LeCun.
