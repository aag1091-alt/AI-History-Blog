Yoshua Bengio, born on March 5, 1964, is a Canadian computer scientist renowned for his pioneering work in artificial neural networks and deep learning. As a professor at the Université de Montréal and co-president of LawZero, Bengio has been instrumental in advancing AI research and education, notably through his contributions to deep learning algorithms and his leadership in establishing Mila.
