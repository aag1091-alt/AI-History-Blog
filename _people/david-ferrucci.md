David Ferrucci is the computer scientist who led one of AI's most dramatic public triumphs: IBM Watson's victory on Jeopardy! in 2011. After nearly two decades at IBM, he founded Elemental Cognition to pursue a new vision of AI grounded in human-like understanding, and later became Managing Director of the Institute for Advanced Enterprise AI.
