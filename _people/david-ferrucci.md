David A. Ferrucci is an American computer scientist renowned for his pivotal role in the creation of IBM Watson, which revolutionized the landscape of artificial intelligence by defeating human champions in Jeopardy!. A graduate of Manhattan College and Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, Ferrucci's career at IBM spanned nearly two decades, during which he spearheaded groundbreaking research and development in knowledge representation and reasoning. Following his IBM tenure, he established Elemental Cognition to explore the innovative field of natural learning, aiming to create AI that comprehends the world as humans do.
