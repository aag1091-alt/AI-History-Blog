David Ferrucci is the computer scientist who proved AI could beat humans at their own trivia game. As the principal investigator behind IBM Watson, he spent four years engineering a system capable of parsing puns, wordplay, and obscure clues well enough to dominate Jeopardy! in a landmark 2011 match. He later founded Elemental Cognition to pursue a deeper vision of machine understanding, arguing that true AI requires genuine comprehension, not just statistical correlation.
