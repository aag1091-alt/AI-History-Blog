David A. Ferrucci is an American computer scientist and entrepreneur renowned for his pivotal role in the creation of IBM's Watson, a groundbreaking AI system that defeated human champions on Jeopardy! Following his tenure at IBM, Ferrucci ventured into founding Elemental Cognition, where he explores natural learning, an innovative approach to AI that seeks to replicate human cognitive processes.
