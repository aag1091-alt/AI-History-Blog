# Her: Falling in Love with an AI
Spike Jonze's 2013 film "Her" introduced an AI named Samantha, who became the first cinematic embodiment of an emotionally intelligent, conversational AI system.

**What happened:** Directed by Spike Jonze and starring Joaquin Phoenix, "Her" was released on December 18, 2013. The film features an AI assistant named Samantha, voiced by Scarlett Johansson, who learns and evolves through conversations with users. Her ability to understand and express complex emotions made her a groundbreaking portrayal of AI.

**Why it matters:** "Her" was prophetic in its depiction of AI as a conversational, emotionally intelligent entity. It anticipated the development of large language models (LLMs) like OpenAI's GPT-4, which can engage in nuanced conversations and learn rapidly. The film's impact is evident in the comparison of GPT-4's voice demo to Samantha, highlighting the film's foresight in AI's emotional and conversational capabilities.
