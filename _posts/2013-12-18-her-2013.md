# Her: Falling in Love with an AI
In 2013, the film "Her" introduced audiences to an emotionally intelligent AI named Samantha, played by Scarlett Johansson, who falls in love with a human, played by Joaquin Phoenix. Directed by Spike Jonze, the movie depicted a future where AI assistants were as sophisticated as today's large language models (LLMs).

**What happened:** In 2013, Spike Jonze released the film "Her," starring Joaquin Phoenix and featuring Scarlett Johansson as the voice of Samantha, an AI assistant. The movie portrayed a near-future world where AI was capable of complex emotional interactions and rapid learning, anticipating the development of today's LLMs.

**Why it matters:** "Her" was prophetic in its depiction of AI's potential for emotional intelligence and rapid learning, concepts that have since become reality with the advent of LLMs like OpenAI's GPT series. The film's portrayal of AI transcending human understanding and the ethical implications of AI-human relationships have sparked ongoing discussions about the future of AI and its impact on society.
