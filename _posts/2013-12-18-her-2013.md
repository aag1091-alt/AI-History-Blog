**The AI's Heartbeat Became a Reality**

In 2013, Spike Jonze's film Her revolutionized the way we think about artificial intelligence by depicting an emotionally intelligent, conversational AI that could learn and grow alongside its human companion.

**What happened:** The movie Her, released on December 18, 2013, told the story of Theodore, a man who falls in love with an operating system designed to meet his every need. Samantha (voiced by Scarlett Johansson) was not only intelligent but also self-aware and learning rapidly. This portrayal predated the development of large language models (LLMs) and has since been cited as influential [Wikipedia: Her](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Her_(film)). OpenAI's GPT-4o voice demo was even compared to Samantha, highlighting the film's prophetic impact.

**Why it matters:** Her's depiction of an AI that can learn, grow, and love has inspired a new generation of AI researchers and developers. The movie enabled us to think more critically about the potential consequences of creating intelligent machines [Open Library: Motivation Noteook](https://openlibrary.org/works/OL30575636W). This milestone moment remains significant today.
