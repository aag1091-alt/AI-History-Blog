**GPT-4 Revolutionizes Multimodal Reasoning**

In March 2023, GPT-4 made history by demonstrating genuine multimodal reasoning, crossing multiple thresholds simultaneously and shifting the debate from 'can AI think?' to 'what can't AI do?'. This breakthrough model, developed by OpenAI's Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, and Ilya Sutskever, surpassed human-level performance in various tasks, including passing the bar exam at the 90th percentile. GPT-4 also scored in the top 10% on the SAT.

**Why it matters:** GPT-4's achievement enabled a new era of multimodal reasoning, pushing the boundaries of what is possible for artificial intelligence. This milestone inspired research and development in areas such as human-AI collaboration, natural language understanding, and cognitive architectures.
