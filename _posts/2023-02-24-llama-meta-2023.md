**The Llama Revolution: Democratizing AI**

In February 2023, Meta AI released the Llama language model, a game-changing AI that would challenge traditional approaches to open-source AI development.

**What happened:** The initial release of Llama was restricted due to its significant size and complexity. However, this decision backfired when unauthorized copies were shared via BitTorrent, sparking an explosion of open-source AI projects like Alpaca, Vicuna, WizardLM, and Mistral. Meta later released instruction fine-tuned versions alongside the foundation model, making it more accessible to researchers and developers.

**Why it matters:** The Llama release marked a turning point in AI development, enabling smaller, open models to rival closed giants in capabilities. As Yann LeCun, Meta's Chief AI Scientist, noted, "Open source AI is the path to democratizing and distributing power in AI." This shift has far-reaching implications for AI research and application.
