# DALL-E 2 & Stable Diffusion: AI Becomes an Artist
In 2022, AI image generation became accessible to the masses with the release of DALL-E 2 and Stable Diffusion, marking a pivotal moment in the evolution of generative AI.

**What happened:** In 2022, OpenAI released DALL-E 2, an advanced text-to-image model developed by Aditya Ramesh, building on the success of the original DALL-E. Around the same time, Robin Rombach and Emad Mostaque at Stability AI introduced Stable Diffusion, a model that democratized AI-generated art. Both tools allowed users to create high-quality images from text descriptions, making AI-generated art accessible to anyone with a browser. [DALL-E](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DALL-E) | [Stable Diffusion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stable_Diffusion)

**Why it matters:** The rapid adoption of DALL-E 2 and Stable Diffusion in 2022 transformed the landscape of AI-generated art. Within months, millions of images were being created daily, sparking debates about copyright, authenticity, and the nature of artistic creation. These tools not only showcased the potential of AI in the creative industry but also raised critical questions about the role of human creativity in the age of artificial intelligence.
