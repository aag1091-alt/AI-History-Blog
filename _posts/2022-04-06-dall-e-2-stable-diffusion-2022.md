**The Dawn of AI Artistry**

In January 2021, OpenAI's DALL-E text-to-image model revolutionized the art world by generating digital images from natural language descriptions, sparking debates about artist copyright and authenticity.

**What happened:** The first version of DALL-E was announced in January 2021, followed by its successor DALL-E 2 in 2022. A paper titled "DALL-E-Bot: Introducing Web-Scale Diffusion Models to Robotics" explored the potential of web-scale diffusion models for robotics, demonstrating zero-shot capabilities in rearranging objects in a scene. [^DALL-E] [^DALL-E-Bot]

**Why it matters:** The DALL-E models enabled AI image generation to move from researchers' labs to the masses, inspiring discussions about what it means to create art and sparking debates about artist copyright. This milestone paved the way for the development of more sophisticated AI models like Stable Diffusion.
