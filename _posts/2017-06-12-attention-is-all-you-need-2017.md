**The Transformer Revolutionized AI Forever**

In 2017, a team of eight scientists at Google published a paper that changed the face of artificial intelligence forever - "Attention Is All You Need," a groundbreaking research paper that introduced the transformer architecture.

**What happened:** The paper built upon the attention mechanism proposed by Bahdanau et al. in 2014 and introduced a new deep learning approach, solving the fundamental bottleneck in sequence processing. The authors tested their Transformer architecture on early examples like English-to-German translation, generating Wikipedia articles, and parsing, convincing them that it was a game-changer.

**Why it matters:** The transformer architecture has become the mainstay of modern language models such as BERT, GPT, T5, Claude, Gemini, and enabled significant advancements in multimodal generative AI. Its impact on AI is undeniable.
