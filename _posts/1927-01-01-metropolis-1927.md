# Metropolis — The First AI Robot on Screen
In 1927, Fritz Lang and his wife Thea von Harbou brought the first AI robot to the silver screen in their groundbreaking film "Metropolis."

**What happened:** In 1927, Fritz Lang directed and Thea von Harbou co-wrote and co-produced the silent film *Metropolis*, which featured a robot named Maria. This film was among the first to explore the concept of artificial intelligence in a dystopian setting. [Metropolis (1927 film)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Metropolis_%281927_film%29)

**Why it matters:** The robot Maria in *Metropolis* became a template for virtually every humanoid robot and android in science fiction that followed. Its design directly influenced characters like C-3PO from *Star Wars* and the Terminator. Moreover, the film's portrayal of AI as a tool of oppression set a precedent for the theme of technology's potential to harm humanity, a concern that remains relevant today.
