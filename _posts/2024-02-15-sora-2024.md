Sora's groundbreaking video clips challenged the entertainment industry's perception of AI capabilities.

**What happened:** In February 2024, OpenAI previewed its text-to-video model, Sora, with a dramatic quality leap from previous video models. Tim Brooks, Bill Peebles, and Sam Altman led the development team. The AI-generated videos generated complex scenes with multiple characters, specific motion types, and accurate details. [Sora (text-to-video model) — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sora_%28text-to-video_model%29)

**Why it matters:** Sora's release accelerated Hollywood studios' consideration of AI in film production, shifting the conversation from image generation to video storytelling. The technology has inspired a new wave of creative possibilities and raised questions about copyright preservation and ownership.
