**The Moment AI Went Mainstream**

On November 30, 2022, ChatGPT crossed the threshold from tool to conversation partner, forcing every company, government, and institution to develop an AI strategy overnight.

**What happened:** ChatGPT was released by OpenAI in November 2022, using generative pre-trained transformers (GPTs) like GPT-5.2. It quickly gained massive popularity, reaching **100 million users in two months**, making it the fastest-growing consumer software application in history.[^ChatGPT] Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, and Ilya Sutskever were key to its development.

**Why it matters:** The release of ChatGPT marked a significant milestone in AI history, accelerating the AI boom and inspiring public debate about the nature of creativity and knowledge work. It also underscored the growing capabilities of AI systems, as noted in a recent study: "ChatGPT is eerily good at this. We are not far from dangerously strong AI."[^ElonMusk]
