# The Chatbot That Broke the Internet (2022)

In 2022, ChatGPT burst onto the scene, becoming the fastest-growing consumer application in history and sparking a global conversation about the future of AI.

**What happened:** In November 2022, OpenAI released ChatGPT, a generative AI chatbot developed by Sam Altman, Ilya Sutskever, and Greg Brockman. Within two months, it had amassed 100 million monthly active users, setting a new record for user growth. [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/technology/chatgpt-sets-record-fastest-growing-user-base-analyst-note-2023-02-01/)

**Why it matters:** ChatGPT's rapid ascent not only underscored the public's growing interest in AI but also catalyzed an industry-wide race to develop more advanced AI technologies. It reshaped perceptions of what machines can achieve, prompting governments worldwide to consider new regulations for AI. The bot's success also highlighted the potential of AI to transform professional fields and challenge traditional notions of creativity and knowledge work.
