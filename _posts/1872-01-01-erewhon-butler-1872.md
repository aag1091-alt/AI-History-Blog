**The Dawn of Consciousness in Machines: Erewhon's Warning**

In 1872, Samuel Butler's science fiction novel "Erewhon" presented a groundbreaking argument for machine consciousness and evolution, eerily anticipating modern AI alignment concerns about superintelligence.

**What happened:** [Darwin Among the Machines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Darwin_Among_the_Machines) proposed that machines could evolve to surpass human intelligence, posing an existential threat. Butler's essay anticipated many of the challenges we face today, including the need for responsible AI development.

**Why it matters:** Erewhon's warnings about machine consciousness enabled future thinkers to consider the long-term implications of creating intelligent beings. The book inspired discussions about AI ethics and the potential consequences of creating superintelligent machines.
