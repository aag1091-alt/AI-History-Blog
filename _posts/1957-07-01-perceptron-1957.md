**The birth of modern AI: Frank Rosenblatt's Perceptron**

In 1957, a team of researchers led by Frank Rosenblatt unveiled the first learning machine, paving the way for the development of neural networks and artificial intelligence.

**What happened:** The Perceptron was a breakthrough in supervised learning, using binary classifiers to predict class labels. Its design laid the foundation for modern linear classification algorithms. [Perceptron — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Perceptron)

**Why it matters:** The Perceptron's impact on AI cannot be overstated. It established the core idea behind every modern neural network, including GPT-4 and beyond, and inspired future generations of researchers.
