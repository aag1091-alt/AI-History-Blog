In 2022, ChatGPT burst onto the scene, captivating the world with its conversational AI prowess and setting a new record for the fastest-growing consumer application. Developed by a dedicated team at OpenAI, ChatGPT did not introduce groundbreaking research but rather fine-tuned GPT-3.5 with reinforcement learning from human feedback, making it accessible and engaging for the public. This development came at a time when AI was already experiencing a surge in investment and public attention, with major milestones like the LIGO and Virgo collaborations' multi-messenger observations of a binary neutron star merger in 2017 highlighting the field's potential.

**What happened:** In 2022, a team at OpenAI released ChatGPT, a fine-tuned version of GPT-3.5 with reinforcement learning from human feedback. The service gained 100 million users in just two months, making it the fastest-growing consumer application in history. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ChatGPT) details how ChatGPT's accessible chat interface transformed public perception of AI overnight.

**Why it matters:** The rapid ascent of ChatGPT in 2022 underscored the transformative power of AI in reshaping industries and public discourse. It compelled major tech companies to accelerate their AI initiatives, highlighting the technology's potential to revolutionize how we interact with information and each other.
