Claude's release marked a significant shift in AI development, prioritizing safety and alignment from the outset.

**What happened:** Claude is a series of large language models developed by Anthropic, founded by former OpenAI researchers Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, Chris Olah, and Jared Kaplan. The model was first released in 2023 and uses constitutional AI, a training technique that improves ethical and legal compliance (AI alignment). [Claude (language model) — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Claude_(language_model))

**Why it matters:** Claude's release enabled a safer approach to AI development, inspiring a focus on constitutional AI. It also showed a credible safety-focused alternative to GPT-4, highlighting the importance of aligning AI with human values.
