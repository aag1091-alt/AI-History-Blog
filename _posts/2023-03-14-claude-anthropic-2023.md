# Claude: Constitutional AI and Safer Assistants
On March 14, 2023, Anthropic released Claude, a language model designed to prioritize safety and ethical compliance over performance.

**What happened:** Anthropic, founded by former OpenAI researchers including Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, Chris Olah, and Jared Kaplan, launched Claude, a large language model that uses constitutional AI. This technique trains the model to adhere to a set of ethical and legal principles. Claude was named after Claude Shannon, the pioneer of information theory. The model's release marked a significant step towards integrating safety features directly into AI systems rather than as an afterthought.

**Why it matters:** Claude's development and release in 2023 highlighted the growing importance of AI safety and ethical considerations in the field of generative AI. By embedding principles of constitutional AI, Anthropic demonstrated a credible approach to aligning AI with human values, setting a new standard for the responsible development of AI technologies.
