Opening sentence capturing why this moment matters.

**What happened:** In 2015, OpenAI was founded by Sam Altman, Elon Musk, Ilya Sutskever, and Greg Brockman as a nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing artificial intelligence in a safe and beneficial way. The group aimed to counter Google's growing AI dominance and ensure that AI technologies were developed for the betterment of humanity.

**Why it matters:** OpenAI's founding marked a pivotal moment in the AI landscape, setting a precedent for transparency and collaboration in the field. Its commitment to sharing research openly and its later pivot to a capped-profit model have made it a central figure in the AI community, influencing the ethical development of AI technologies.
