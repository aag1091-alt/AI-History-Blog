# OpenAI: A Billion-Dollar Bet on Safe Superintelligence
In 2015, a group of tech luminaries, including Elon Musk and Sam Altman, founded OpenAI as a nonprofit dedicated to advancing safe artificial intelligence. [1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OpenAI)

**What happened:** On December 11, 2015, OpenAI was established in Delaware as a nonprofit organization by Elon Musk, Sam Altman, Ilya Sutskever, Greg Brockman, and others. The founding members aimed to ensure that AI research would be conducted openly and for the benefit of humanity, rather than being monopolized by a few large corporations like Google. [1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OpenAI)

**Why it matters:** OpenAI's founding marked a significant moment in the history of AI, as it set the stage for a new era of open-source AI research. Over the years, OpenAI has become one of the most influential AI organizations, known for its groundbreaking work on language models like GPT and image generation models like DALL-E. Despite its initial nonprofit status, OpenAI later transitioned to a capped-profit model, which has led to both controversy and significant advancements in the field. [1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OpenAI)
