**The moment AI went from geeky novelty to mainstream phenomenon.**

In 2011, IBM's Watson system made history by defeating human contestants on the popular game show Jeopardy! [Watson (computer)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Watson_(computer)). On February 16, 2011, Watson's natural language processing skills were put to the test against Ken Jennings and Brad Rutter. The outcome was a resounding win, cementing AI's place in the public imagination.

**Why it matters:** Watson's Jeopardy victory marked a turning point for AI, enabling the development of more conversational interfaces like Siri and Alexa. It also inspired a new wave of natural language processing research, paving the way for advancements like ChatGPT.
