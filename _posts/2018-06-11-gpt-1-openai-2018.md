**The Groundbreaking GPT-1 Model Revolutionized AI**

In June 2018, OpenAI's Generative Pre-trained Transformer 1 (GPT-1) made history by introducing a paradigm shift in artificial intelligence with its 'pretraining + fine-tuning' approach. This innovative method allowed for the efficient scaling up of language models like GPT-2 and GPT-3.

**What happened:** [OpenAI released GPT-1, a generative pre-trained transformer-based language model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GPT-1), marking a significant departure from supervised learning methods that relied on large amounts of manually labeled data. Alec Radford, Karthik Narasimhan, Tim Salimans, and Ilya Sutskever were the key people behind this groundbreaking model.

**Why it matters:** GPT-1's success enabled the development of more powerful language models like ChatGPT, which can generalize to a wide range of tasks. This achievement also paved the way for the creation of multimodal AI models that can understand and process different types of data.
