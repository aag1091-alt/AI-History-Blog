**A New Power Player Emerges in AI: DeepSeek Challenges Silicon Valley's Dominance**

DeepSeek, a Chinese AI company founded by Liang Wenfeng and funded by the hedge fund High-Flyer, has made headlines with its launch of the DeepSeek-R1 model. Released in January 2025, this large language model proved that AI progress is not limited by compute power alone, challenging Silicon Valley's long-held narrative about the need for high-performance hardware.

[DeepSeek — Wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DeepSeek](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DeepSeek)
The DeepSeek-R1 model's training cost was significantly lower than other LLMs, including OpenAI's GPT-4. This achievement triggered an "AI Sputnik moment," with US AI commentators calling it a wake-up call for the industry.

[Quantitative Analysis of Performance Drop in DeepSeek Model Quantization (arXiv): http://arxiv.org/abs/2505.02390v2](http://arxiv.org/abs/2505.02390v2)
The launch of DeepSeek-R1 marked a significant shift in the AI landscape, enabling local deployment and accelerating the geopolitical AI race.
